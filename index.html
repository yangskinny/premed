Memm Anki Deck

Memm is Kevin Jubbal's (aka MedSchoolInsiders) Anki/Quizlet clone. I turned all the flashcards from this website into an Anki deck because let's face it: Memm still falls short of Anki. There's no dark mode, it's limited in customization, you can't mass suspend cards, and the new algorithm he advertises is BS. Anki also has the new FSRS, so there's really no need to shell out on Memm's supposed superior algorithm. However, credit where credit is due, I do actually think the cards are pretty well-written. So check it out. Maybe use it to supplement your content review. Everything is tagged, and I even tagged all the equations. I included an "Extra" section in the behavioral sciences which contains the new AnkingxPankow PS cards, as well as some of my own while reviewing UWorld. Memm's Behavioral Sciences cards were supposedly made using the 300-page doc, but there were a lot of things they excluded, that the AnkingxPankow cards will cover. And you'll hear people complaining how Pankow still doesn't cover everything. Well, hopefully the UWorld stuff I've added will address the gaps.
